Yves

It/It - Synthetic Rodent

Yves is a small synthetic rodent that lives in a datacenter, It's made of a highly durable rubber that can squeeze between hard drives and other tight spaces.

Whenever Yves isn't in need it gets comfortable and sleeps between the wires.
While it sleeps, it curiously browses the files and databases on the servers.
